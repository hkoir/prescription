🧒 Child Safety & CSAE Prevention – AIHA Prescription App

Effective Date: June 19, 2025

At AIHA Prescription, we are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users, especially children. We take the prevention of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) seriously and implement strict content and usage policies to support this goal.

🔐 Our Commitments

  • Zero Tolerance for CSAE: We do not allow any content or behavior that exploits or harms children.
  • Content Responsibility: We moderate and review content to ensure it remains safe and appropriate.
  • Age Appropriateness: Our app and services are not directed at children under the age of 13.
  • User Reporting: Users can report concerns or inappropriate content via email: humayun@e2esolutionsbd.com
  • Legal Compliance: We comply with all applicable national and international child protection laws.
